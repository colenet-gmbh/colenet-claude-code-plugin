## Matt Pocock — [`mattpocock/skills`](https://github.com/mattpocock/skills) (MIT)

Skills ported from or inspired by Matt Pocock's repo (© 2026 Matt Pocock). MIT text below.

- `grill-me`, `grill-with-docs`, `grilling` — the relentless interview plus the
  glossary/ADR discipline (from `grill-me`, `grill-with-docs`, `domain-modeling`).
- `split` — from `to-issues`
- `triage` — from `triage`; adapted to the `docs/work/` board (state in frontmatter, rejected requests in `docs/work/out-of-scope/`), no tracker/PR handling.
- `implement`, `build`, `tdd` — the TDD / implement discipline from `tdd`, `implement`.
- `diagnosing-bugs` — from `diagnosing-bugs`; handoff retargeted to `architect`.
- `architect` — codebase and domain design plus deepening, from `codebase-design`,
  `domain-modeling`, `improve-codebase-architecture`.
- `prototype` — from `prototype`.
- `research` — from `research` (v1.1.0); the background-agent reading-legwork discipline
  adopted faithfully, then adapted to cape: the note's location coupled to the repo's
  `CONTEXT.md` convention, the description rewritten to distinguish it from `deep-research`,
  German + English trigger phrases added, and a `grill-with-docs`-feeder framing.
- `teach` — from `teach` (v1.1.0); frontmatter adapted to cape conventions (German +
  English trigger phrases), otherwise adopted as-is.
- `handoff` — from Matt Pocock's `handoff` skill.
- `writing-great-skills`
- `ask-cape` — router pattern inspired by `ask-matt`.
